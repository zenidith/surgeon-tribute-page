Surgeon

Surgeon is the pseudonym of Anthony Child, an English electronic musician and DJ.

Highlights from Surgeon's life:

  • 1971 - Tony was born in Northamptonshire, UK.
  • 1989 - He moved to Birmingham to study audio-visual design, played in a jazz/rock/fusion band called 'Blim', and learned to DJ from friend Paul Damage.
  • 1992 - Started DJing at House of God, a local techno event
  • 1994 - Released his eponymous debut EP on Downwards Records.
  • 1996 - Released his debut LP 'Communications', also on Downwards.
  • 1998 - Released his third LP 'Balance' on Tresor.
  • 2007 - Performed at The Labyrinth festival in Japan for the first time.
  • 2013 - Release 'The Space Between People And Things' under his own name, Anthony Child.
  • 2021 - Surgeon turns 50 years old.

"Expecting me to remember what gig I play on which day and where is akin to asking me how a cup of tea tasted 5 years ago."

— Surgeon
